About CTK Restaurant

Located in the heart of Coimbatore, CTK Restaurant is your destination for a rich culinary experience. Established in November 2019, we offer a diverse menu featuring authentic South Indian, North Indian, and continental dishes. With a live kitchen counter, spacious seating for over 90 guests, and a commitment to using fresh, quality ingredients without artificial additives, we ensure a memorable dining experience. Enjoy our dine-in, takeaway, and delivery services, along with exceptional buffet options. Join us at CTK Restaurant, where flavour and quality meet.

About CTK Banquet Restaurant

Situated just above CTK Restaurant in Coimbatore, our elegant banquet hall is the perfect venue for your special events. With a capacity starting from 40 guests, the air-conditioned and spacious hall provides a comfortable and stylish setting for any occasion. An ample parking, a dedicated event team, and a lavish buffet with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options tailored to your preferences. At CTK Banquet Hall, we ensure your event is memorable and seamlessly executed.
